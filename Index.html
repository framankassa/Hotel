About Evergreen Hotel

Nestled in the heart of Addis Ababa, Evergreen Hotel offers a tranquil retreat with breathtaking views of the city and surrounding mountains. Since our opening in 2010, we have been committed to providing exceptional service and unforgettable experiences to our guests.

Our hotel combines contemporary design with traditional Ethiopian elements, creating a unique atmosphere that celebrates local culture while offering all the modern amenities discerning travelers expect.

Whether you're visiting for business or leisure, our dedicated team is here to ensure your stay is nothing short of perfect.
