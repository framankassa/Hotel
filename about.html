Our Story

Discover the legacy of Evergreen Hotel

Welcome to Evergreen Hotel

Founded in 2010, Evergreen Hotel has established itself as one of Addis Ababa's premier hospitality destinations. Our name reflects our commitment to sustainability and our lush, green surroundings that provide a serene escape in the heart of the city.

From our humble beginnings as a small boutique hotel, we've grown into a 150-room luxury property while maintaining our personal touch and commitment to exceptional service.

Our Mission

To provide an unforgettable experience that blends Ethiopian hospitality with world-class service, creating lasting memories for our guests.

Our Values

  • Commitment to excellence in all we do
  • Respect for our guests, employees, and community
  • Sustainable operations that protect our environment
  • Celebration of Ethiopian culture and heritage
